# Sample Prompts

Use these after uploading and indexing a few PDFs.

## General Q&A

- What is the main problem this document is trying to solve?
- Summarize the methodology in three bullet points.
- What limitations or risks are mentioned?
- Which assumptions does the author make?
- Compare the proposed approach with the baseline.

## Citation Checks

- Which page discusses the experiment setup?
- Where does the document mention evaluation metrics?
- Give me the answer with page citations.

## Multilingual

- इस दस्तावेज़ में मुख्य निष्कर्ष क्या हैं?
- ¿Cuáles son las limitaciones mencionadas en el documento?
- Résume les résultats principaux du document.

## Conflict Checker

- Do both documents agree about the main benefits?
- Compare their claims about privacy risk.
- Do the two reports contradict each other on cost estimates?
